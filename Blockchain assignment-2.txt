Q.4  What is the difference between centralised vs decentralised blockchain?
Ans. In the centralised blockchain there is one central or main entity control majority of blockchain and has majority power with it. While in the decentralized 
     blockchain there are nodes and all nodes on blockchain has whole copy of blockchain.The voting occurs in decision making and majority votes win.There is no 
     one centrally controlling it.Example of decentralized blockchain is BITCOIN.
